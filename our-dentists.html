Meet Our Dentists

Meet Smile Again Dentistry crew–our space feels both familiar and inviting, our crew seriously knows their stuff, and our approach promotes long-term luster over emergency care

Maison Cyr

Certifications and Qualifications

Dr. Mason Cyr is a Smile Again native, born and raised in Nairobi,Kenya. He attended University of New England and received a bachelors degree in Applied Exercise Science. His focus shifted from sports medicine to dentistry when he had a sport related injury and disliked his recovery process wherein he found his passion for dentistry. Becoming a Dentist from that point meant more than making beautiful smiles, dentistry means involvement in the community and providing additional services to those in need

Dr.Leo

Certifications and Qualifications

Dr. Leo graduated from Havard University and is a fellow with the International College of Oral Implantologists. He is proud to be named among Kenya's Top Dentists in 2021, 2022 and 2023 and to bring his expertise in family and cosmetic dental services to our beloved country.Before becoming a dentist, Dr. Leo served in the military and spent over ten years in the Air Force as an avionics specialist and then dental director. During his military tenure, he served overseas on two deployments and received numerous awards including Airman of the Year. Dr. Leo continues to serve by providing free dental care to kids and families on service trips all over the country.At Smile Again Dentistry, Dr. Leo has established a unique approach focused on comfort, quality, and no judgment experiences. Dr. Leo knew he wanted to become a dentist since he was a kid, and wants everyone to love their dental experiences as much as he did growing up. When he isn’t working on happy smiles, he can be found hiking Cadillac mountain, white water rafting, kiteboarding, or skiing.
